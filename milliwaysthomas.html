About
"Milliways, better known as the Restaurant at the End of the Universe, is a five star restaurant situated at the end of time and matter. Its main attraction is allowing diners to view a Gnab Gib, before desserts are served. The Restaurant has some of the most staggeringly extravagant decor ever seen, a variety of the strangest guests from throughout history, and serves a particularly fine Pan Galactic Gargle Blaster. Milliways is run by an incredible arrangement of life forms from everywhen, and is the one place anywhen that serves talking food."
Android Mk II 'TV'
Head Chef
The Android Robot Chef, 'TV' produces some of the most beautiful food that Milliways has to offer. He first started working at the restauraunt 3 billion years ago and has been working here as the Head Chef since. Not once has he malfunctioned and produced food anything less than perfectly. Having him as part of the team has helped get the other chefs to work more effeciently and we are delighted to have him onboard for another 5 billion years until his model of androird will have to be replaced.
Mellisa Wal
Assistant Head Chef
Chef Mellisa is here at Milliways temporarily, working as the head chefs assistant. Being a humanoid, she will unfortunately not be with us for long and her skills will be surely missed. Since coming on as assistant head chef she has worked non stop to make sure the standard of food produced meets the quality of Milliways high expectation that its customers come from all over the universe to expect. We pride our selves on great food and Mellisa makes well sure of that.
